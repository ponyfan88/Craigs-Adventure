Meet Craig

Craig is the perfect Crazy Uncle. Hip clothes, side burns, questionable intellect. He really has it all! Nobody knows how he ended up in the land of Jauntlet, but he sure isn't going to stick around to find out.

The Land of Jauntlet

The land of Jauntlet is a mysterious land cursed with all sorts of magic. From Goblins to wizards and slimes, anything could happen in the land of Jauntlet.

Your Weapons

Craig is not unarmed during his journey. Craig always carries around his trusty sword in case of flying squirrels, and it's paid off cause not only can he deal damage close range, but also get away quickly with his dash.

Your Tools

Unkept dungeons are always filled with junk sitting in every corner. Thankfully Craig is very resourceful as he will gladly pick up strange statues and ominous runes as to throw them at the nearest moving object. Watch for bombs and heart containers as they will show themselves very useful.
